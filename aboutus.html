PURPOSE OF CANDOR & CHARACTER

Candor & Character offers an entertaining approach to creating a Dungeons & Dragons character using the Wizards Of The Coast character sheet. The twist, hence the word candor, is that the character's stats are derived from a series of quizzes that you answer in an honest and/or fun way. So, don’t take it too seriously. The point is to have fun and create a silly character that you wouldn't otherwise choose. So which character are you based on our questionnaire? Take the quiz and find out. If you've already taken the quiz, invite your friends to try and compare your results!
