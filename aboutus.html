PURPOSE OF CANDOR & CHARACTER

The purpose of candor & character was to create a dungeon and dragons character using the wizards of the coast character sheet. The twist, hence the word candor, is to create a random character from a series of quizzes and us judging you in a honest and fun way. So, don’t take it too seriously. The point is to have fun and create a silly character that you wouldn't otherwise choose. So which character are you based on our questionnaire? Take the quiz and find out. If you've already taken the quiz, print it out and show your friends. Then tell them to create their own character by visiting our website.
